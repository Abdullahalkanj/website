Awards and Recognition

"The Lord of the Rings" trilogy received numerous awards, including 17 Academy Awards in total: "The Fellowship of the Ring" won 4, "The Two Towers" won 2, and "The Return of the King" won 11, including Best Picture.

Critical Acclaim

IMDb Ratings: Each film holds an 8.7+ rating out of 10.

Rotten Tomatoes: Each film has over 90% on the Tomatometer.

Top 100 Lists: "The Return of the King" is listed among the top 100 films on several notable film industry lists.
